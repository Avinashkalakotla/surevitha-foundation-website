About Us

Purpose-Driven.
Community-First.

Surevitha Foundation is a grassroots-level preventive healthcare organization committed to transforming how India approaches early detection and health awareness.

In a country where thousands lose their lives due to late diagnosis and lack of awareness, we believe that bridging the gap between communities and healthcare is the most urgent mission of our time.

Led by Suman Velpula, our team combines on-ground execution expertise with trusted medical partnerships and cutting-edge AI screening tools to create scalable, life-saving impact.

🎯 Our Mission

Screen 100,000 people across underserved communities through awareness, community-based screening, and accessible healthcare programs.

🌐 Our Vision

A world where no life is lost due to lack of awareness, late detection, or limited access to preventive healthcare.

What We Do

Structured Programs.
Measurable Lives Saved.

We design and execute scalable preventive healthcare programs delivered directly within underserved communities across India.

🎗️

Breast Cancer Early Detection

Clinical examination and AI-based screening to detect breast cancer at an early, treatable stage — our flagship program that has identified hundreds of high-risk cases.

📢

Community Health Awareness

Mobilizing underserved populations through local networks, educating communities about prevention, symptoms, and the importance of timely screening.

❤️

Basic Life Support (BLS) Training

Equipping community members and frontline workers with life-saving skills — CPR, emergency response, and first aid — to act in critical moments.

🩺

General Health Screening Camps

Blood pressure, diabetes, and other essential health checks delivered directly to communities that lack access to routine medical care.

🔗

Referral & Follow-up Support

High-risk cases don't fall through the cracks. We provide structured referral pathways and follow-up support to ensure early detection leads to timely treatment.
